Fried Chicken

Chicken tenders, wings, and drums breaded, battered, and fried. This recipe is an all time classic and should be known by everyone.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Flour
  • Canola Oil
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Eggs

Directions

  1. Beat 3 eggs in a bowl for the eggwash, set aside
  2. Pour flour in a seperate bowl, add a dash of garlic powder, onion powder, salt and pepper
  3. Dredge chicken in eggwash, then cover in flour, put back in the eggwash again, then cover in flour once more
  4. Set aside the battered chicken
  5. Bring pot of oil to 375 degrees fahrenheight
  6. Dip a few pieces of chicken in the oil at a timeand cook for 6 to ten minutes each batch
  7. Remove chicken from oil and set aside
  8. Serve and enjoy
