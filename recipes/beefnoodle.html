Beef Noodle

A low-mein based beef dish with green onions and garlic. This dish hits all the right spots on the sweet and savory palette.

Ingredients

  • Low-mein noodles
  • Garlic
  • Scallions
  • Butter
  • Soy Sauce
  • Brown Sugar
  • Beef

Directions

  1. Start boiling a pot of water
  2. Dice scallion and garlic, set aside.
  3. Trim fat off beef, cut into cubes, set aside
  4. When water is boiling, add low-mein and cook for 4 minutes, then drain and put aside
  5. Heat pan over medium heat, add butter
  6. When butter is melted, add diced scallion and garlic, cook until fragrent about 1 minute.
  7. Add cubed beef and cook for 2 minutes, stirring often
  8. Add brown sugar to pan, stir in and coat the beef
  9. Add the soy sauce, cook for addition 2 minutes or until sauce has thickened and beef is cooked
  10. Add noodles to pan and stir, coating noodles with sauce
  11. Serve and enjoy
