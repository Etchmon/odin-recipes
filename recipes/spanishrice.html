Spanish Rice

Spanish rice is a slighty spicy, slightly tangy rice with deep flavors. Goes well with chicken, beef or fish and is perfect for tacos.

  • Rice
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Chicken Stock
  • Salsa
  • Cilantro
  • Olive oil
  1. Dice oinion and garlic, set aside
  2. Add 2 tablespoons of Olive oil to pan and put on medium heat
  3. When pan is hot, add onion and garlic, cook until fragrant, 1 - 2 minutes
  4. Add rice to pan, cook until slightly brown, 1 - 2 minutes
  5. Add chicken stock and bring to a simmer, cook 15-20 minutes or until stock is absorbed by the rice
  6. Chop cilantro and spring ontop of rice
  7. Serve and enjoy
